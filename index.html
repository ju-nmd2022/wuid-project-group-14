Your guide to London!

London is one of the world's biggest cities, being England's capital city and home to over 8,5 million people. The city was first known as Londinium when founded by the Romans in 47 A.D. Since modern times London has been quite famous for its amount of international tourism which has led to over 300 languages being spoken in the city. The capital is filled with historical buildings and architecture such as Big Ben, Tower Bridge and Buckingham palace. During its expansion, it has also been filled with more modern architecture that has become famous such as The Shard, Gherkin and London Eye.

Being such a big and international city has had many influences on education and art, being home to some of the highest-ranked academic institutions in the world as well as to some of the most famous art pieces throughout history. The iconic Underground is also located in London and is one of the oldest rapid transit systems in the world and the busiest transporting over 1 million people every day to different locations in London.
