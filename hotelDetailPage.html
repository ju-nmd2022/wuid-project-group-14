The Tower Hotel

This hotel is located in London City Centre. It is only a 3-minuts walk from Tower Bridge and 8 minuts by foot from Tower of London. Here you can stay in one of 801 guestrooms. Every room has television. Housekeeping is provided daily. There is a fitness centre available on the hotel. You can also take in the view for our beeutiful terrace.

Facilities

Non-smoking rooms, fitness center, restaurant, room/facilities for people with disabilities, room service, parking on site, free Wi-Fi, family room, bar, good breakfast
